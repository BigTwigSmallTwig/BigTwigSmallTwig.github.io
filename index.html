Project: Gamer's Nook

Features we would like to offer:

Saving a list/lists of games the user would like to play:

  • Keep track of the games you have on your wishlists from different platforms
  • Allow you to filter you list/lists to view games that fit criteria of your choosing
  • Randomly select a game for you based on criteria of your choosing
  • Allow you to add tags to the games in your list that can be used as custom criteria for the previous two features listed

Customizable Profile:

  • Give you statistics about your gamin history and the games you've played
  • Allow you to display information about yourself in the form of widgets (i.e. favorite game, game you've spent the most time on, etc.)
  • In-site badges that will be awarded to you once you reach certain milestones
  • Recommend games that other users with similar gaming history like

For completionists:

  • Having a list of trophies/achievements for all games listed on the website
  • Tagging trophies of certain types and allowing their use as filtering criteria
  • Allowing you to connect your PS/XBox/Steam accounts to have your trophy/achievement information present
  • Give you statistics about your trophy/achievement hunting history

Our Goals:

We want to prioritize the user experience and plan to have an open line of communication between us and the users. Examples of this is putting out suveys before making any changes and taking suggestions of improvements we can make.
